writing without friction.

sometimes you just want to writ you don't want to see any text other than what you're writing. you want to have a blank canvas where your thoughts can just flow.

we've had apps like this but at the end of the day when they get bigger, things get messy with the ui. Notion was one and now seems i need a course to learn how to write properly on Notion because rn it's just more than a writing app and tbh, even after watching these tutorials, i don't still know how too write without being distracted on notion.

so here's ecrit
